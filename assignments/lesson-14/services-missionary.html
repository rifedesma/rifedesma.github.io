At Temple Inn and Suites we make a special effort to accomodate temple service missionaries who may need to join us for extended stays. Each location has a few laptops available for use in our spacious lobbies. There is also printing services available.

There is a coin operated laundry facility on each floor of the hotel for your convience.

Our "kitchenette" suites are equiped with amenities for a longer stay, such as a stove, full size fridge/freezer, cabnit space, dishwasher, place settings for four people, two medium pots and two medium pans. You will also find a desk in each room perfect for scripture study or tending to any business.
