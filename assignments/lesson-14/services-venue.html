Each of our locations is equiped with a modest reception hall that can sit up to 300 people. The areas are clean and very easy to customize.

Catering options are available. Each guest has a choice between chicken or salmon, and can choose two side options from the following selection: steamed vegetables, wild rice, hot roll with butter, mashed potato, baked potato, or a house salad.

Each location also has a kitchen available at no extra cost to the family and outside catering should you choose to provide your own catering.
